1. Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how BAO Solutions ("BAO", "we", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our websites, products, and services.

2. Data we collect

Information you provide

  • Account data (name, email, company, role)
  • Billing data (billing contact, payment information handled by our payment provider)
  • Support requests and communications

Information collected automatically

  • Usage logs, device and browser information, IP address, timestamps
  • Product telemetry such as feature usage and performance signals
  • Cookies and similar technologies (see Cookies section)

Third‑party sources

  • Integrations you connect (e.g., analytics, ad platforms, email providers)
  • Public or partner-provided datasets as authorized by you

3. How we use data

  • Provide and operate the Services, including authentication and account management
  • Deliver insights, activations, and AI features you request
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot issues
  • Improve product performance and develop new features
  • Communicate with you about updates, security notices, and support
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Lawful bases

Where required (e.g., GDPR), we process data based on one or more lawful bases: performance of a contract, legitimate interests (e.g., securing and improving the Service), legal obligation, and consent where applicable.

5. Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide the Service and for legitimate business or legal purposes. Retention periods vary by data type and use.

6. Sharing & processors

We may share data with trusted service providers (processors) who act on our behalf under data processing agreements, and with third parties when required by law or with your consent. We do not sell personal data.

7. Security

We implement technical and organizational safeguards to protect data, including encryption in transit, access controls, logging, and regular reviews.

8. International transfers

Where data is transferred internationally, we rely on appropriate safeguards (e.g., SCCs, adequacy decisions) as required by applicable laws.

9. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, rectify, delete, restrict, or object to processing of personal data, request portability, and withdraw consent. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@baosolutions.ai.

10. Cookies

We use cookies to operate the site, remember preferences, and measure performance. You can control cookies via your browser settings. Where required, we will request consent for non‑essential cookies.

11. Children’s data

Our Services are not directed to children under 16. If we learn we have collected such data, we will delete it.

12. Updates

We may update this Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Material changes will be highlighted on this page with an updated date.

13. Contact

Questions about this Policy can be sent to support@baosolutions.ai.
